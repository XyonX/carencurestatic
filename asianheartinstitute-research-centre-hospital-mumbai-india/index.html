Asian Heart Institute


Asian Heart Institute in association with the Vattikuti Foundation introduces Asian Vattikuti Institute of Robotic Surgery. Asian Vattikuti Institute of Robotic Surgery brings Robotic Surgery to Mumbai. With the da Vinci Si Robotic Surgical System with simulator, the only one of its kind in India, the Institute is set to become the leader for robotic surgery in the city.

Vision of Asian Heart Institute

To operate as a world - class heart hospital, incorporating the latest technological advances and ethical practices to provide quality heart care at reasonable cost


Achievements of AHI in the last eight years

India's Highest Accredited Hospital with JCI, NIAHO & ISO certifications Over 1,00,000 patients treated by AHI Doctors in over 8 years. One of the lowest surgical mortality rates in the world, 0.26% in isolated bypass surgeries & an overall mortality of 0.8%. Over 9,000 cardiac surgeries performed in last 8 years. AHI have so far performed over 20,000 angiographies and over 5000 interventional procedures, which include complex coronary as well as non-coronary intervention. Amongst the lowest documented infection rate in India which is on par with the best in the world One of the few centres in the world doing nearly 100% of bypass surgeries on a beating heart & with a very significant percentage using total arterial grafting. Only comprehensive Pediatric Care Centre in Western India'. Was the team specially chosen for Hon'ble Prime Minister Dr. Manmohan Singh's redo bypass surgery & post operative care
