1) What Should You Know About Urethroplasty?

Urethroplasty or Urethral Surgery is a surgical reconstruction procedure to fix the urethra that has been narrowed by scar tissues and urethral stricture in the urine channel. Urethroplasty gives the best and most durable results to treat and manage all aspects of urethral diseases and conditions. The urethra is reconstructed to cure the defect or the injury of urethral strictures. There are some of the problems related to the urethra, that can occur due to-

  • Any injury of the defect in the urethra
  • Infection
  • Trauma
  • Iatrogenic injury

2) When You Need A Urethroplasty Surgery?

You may feel some of the signs and symptoms that you should consider as the warning signs. Consult the doctor and discuss the problems with him so that he may suggest proper treatment.

  • Slow urine stream or pain with urinating
  • You may feel that all urine does not come out
  • Some may have no ability to urinate
  • Bladder infection
  • Blood or dark urine
  • Loss of bladder control
  • Swelling of the penis
