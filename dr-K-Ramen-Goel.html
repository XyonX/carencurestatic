Dr. K. Ramen Goel

  
   

Laparoscopy & Bariatric Surgeon
MBBS, MS, FICS(USA)

Dr Ramen Goel is experienced advanced laparoscopy surgeon. He has trained several surgical post-graduates in laparoscopic surgery. He is pioneer bariatric surgeon in India, having performed first lap gastric band surgery & first banded gastric bypass (Fobi pouch) surgeries in India. He has been trained in bariatric surgeries at France & USA.

Dr Ramen Goel brings to Wockhardt Hospital a vast experience of performing thousands of advanced laparoscopic surgeries including bariatric surgeries - lap gastric band, lap gastric bypass, sleeve gastrectomy, banded gastric bypass(Fobi pouch) & intra gastric balloons.

Professional Qualifications & Fellowships:

  • 1986 MBBS, J N Medical College, Aligarh, India
  • 1989 MS(General Surgery), J N Medical College, India
  • 1993 Diploma(I) in Abdominal Surgery, Postgraduate Medical Institute, University of Vienna, Austria
  • Lap Gastric Band Surgery – Dr V Frering, Lyon, France
  • Lap Gastric Bypass – Dr Michel Suter, Laussane, Switzerland
  • Lap Banded Gastric Bypass – Dr Mathias Fobi, LA, USA
  • Preceptor of American Society of Bariatric Surgeons, LA, USA

Fellowships :

  • Obesity : International Federation for Surgery of Obesity
  • Gastro-intestinal Surgery : International College of Surgeons, Chicago, USA

Honours, Presentations & Publications :

  • Honorary Associate Professor of Surgery, Grant Medical College
  • Invited faculty at international & national bariatric surgery meetings
  • Contributed chapter on Recent Advances in obesity in book on
  • Reproductive Endocrinology
