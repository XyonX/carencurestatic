Dr. Kedar Ganla
Consultant - Fertility Physician
Coordinator - IVF Department at Dr.L H Hiranandani Hospital

Qualifications

MBBS - Seth Lokmanya Tilak Medical College and Sion Hospital, University of Mumbai in 1990 MD (Obstetrics and Gynecology) - Seth G S Medical College and Wadia Maternity Hospital, University of Mumbai in 1995.

Biosketch

Dr Kedar Ganla is working at Dr L H Hiranandani Hospital as a Visiting Consultant Fertility Physician since April 2007.

He was awarded scholarship by Canadian International Development Agency (CIDA) and Federation of Fertility Societies (IFFS) and was given a travelling fellowship to Royal Victoria Hospital at Montreal.

He was awarded the MOGS Pramila Bhatia Young Scientific award 2008 for his path breaking work on 'Impairment of endometrial and subendometrial blood flow in women with unexplained infertility'

He has published more than 25 papers in national as well as international journals. He has also presented many papers at various Conferences and received many prizes for his original pioneering work in the field of infertility.

He has worked with thousands of infertile patients over the last decade using a combination of attentive personal care and advanced medical technology giving very good results.

He has specialized in treating couples with challenging problems like male infertility, women with diminished ovarian reserve, decreased sub endometrial blood flow, failure of implantation, genital tuberculosis, women with advancing age, recurrent IVF Failures, surrogacy etc.

His holistic approach towards treatment includes not only medical therapy but also lifestyle modification like stress management, obesity management to improve the IVF success rates.
