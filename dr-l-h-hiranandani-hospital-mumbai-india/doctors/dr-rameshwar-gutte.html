Biosketch

Dr Rameshwar Gutte is working as a Consultant Dermatologist at Dr L H Hiranandani Hospital since October 2011

Dr Rameshwar Gutte has obtained special training and developed his area of interest in 'Dermato-pathology' which helps for accurate diagnosis of skin disease. During his postgraduate course, he had the opportunity to work in the field of phototherapy for various dermatoses like psoriasis and vitiligo, dermatopathology, pediatric dermatology and cosmetology apart from clinical dermatology.

He has more than 30 publications of clinical cases in national and international journals to his credit. He has presented scientific papers in various conferences at state and national level.

He is also a winner of Prestigious Dr. V. R. Mehta Award. He received Special Appreciation Award for Poster presentation in DERMACON 2010 at Lucknow for poster presentation. He was awarded prize for poster presentation at International symposium of Dermatopathology in 2011 at Banglore.

His field of interest includes clinical dermatology and therapeutics and dermato-pathology.
