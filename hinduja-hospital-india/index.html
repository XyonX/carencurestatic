Doctors at Hinduja hospital
Dr. Manju Butani, M.D, graduated from the anesthesiology-training program of Seth G. S. Medical College and KEM group of hospitals, Mumbai, in 1981. Dr. Butani is currently working as the Head of Department of Anesthesiology and Operating rooms at the prestigious multi-specialty tertiary care, Hinduja Hospital and Research Centre, Mumbai. She is the past-president of Research Society of Anesthesiology and clinical pharmacology (RSACP), Indian Society of Cardiovascular Thoracic Anesthesiology (IACTA), the Mumbai chapter of Indian Society of Anesthesia (ISA). She was also representing the Indian group at some of the World congress in Anesthesiology. She has actively and successfully conducted international conferences and workshops, to promote education in the field of Anesthesiology in India. Her special interests have ranged from Cardiac anesthesia, Orthopedics and Pain Management. Hinduja Hospital was amongst the earliest to have an Acute and Chronic Pain service. She has been actively involved in the design and development of Operation Theatre and intensive care units. She has presented papers and participated as faculty at various national and international conferences.

Stay Services
All category of surgeries which require only a day stay are done in Short Stay Services which can belong to Minor, Intermediate, Major, Major Plus category The Short Stay Services department is located on the fourth floor of the hospital building. It is a 19-bedded ward. The department is open from 7.00 a.m. to 8.00 p.m. in the evening except Holidays and Sundays.
