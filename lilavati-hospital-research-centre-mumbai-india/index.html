Medical Insurance

Dr. Ranjit Pandey MD.
Dr. Ranjit Pandey MD has been in the field of the Administration of Hospitals and of Health Care Projects since the last 20 years. He has been very closely associated with Medical Insurance from its very beginning. In the early nineties when Mediclaim was opened to private operators he was involved in the creation of several types of Medical Insurance Packages for various hospitals. During his career in Hospital Administration he has been responsible for creating public awareness with respect to Medical Insurance. More recently he has been advising "Private Insurance" companies regarding the most appropriate "Insurance Packages" for the India. Dr Ranjit Pandey is a Medical Graduate from the Prince of Wales Medical College, Patna from where he also did his MD and DCH. He has worked with TISCO at the Tata Main Hospital in Jamshedpur for 13 years and later with the Apollo Hospital at Hyderabad. This followed a string of attachments to various hospitals and Health Care projects in Hyderabad, Chennai, Bangalore, Gwalior, Noida, Varanasi etc. He had held the position of Executive Director of the Singapore based Health care Company, Medi - Projects Pte Ltd before taking the current assignment with a leading private hospital-Lilavati in Mumbai. Dr. Pandey has a lot of experience from the Health Provider and the Patient's point of view as well, with respect to Medical Insurance.

Lilavati Kirtilal Mehta Medical Trust

The Lilavati Hospital is the creation of the Lilavati Kirtilal Mehta Medical Trust. Formed in 1978, the Lilavati Kirtilal Mehta Medical Trust has engaged in charitable endeavors across India. Members take personal initiative every year to distribute clothes and medicine to the poor in rural areas and have successfully set up medical clinics in many remote and ill-equipped hamlets of India. The Trust now broadens its sphere of activity by addressing one of the most fundamental rights of every human being: the right to live in good health. In a country where thousands die each day due to their inability to access an expensive health care system the Lilavati Kirtilal Mehta Trust seeks to advance the frontiers of specialized medical care and evaluate new techniques designed for a quality health care delivery system. The combination of research and education with outpatient and hospital care distinguishes the Lilavati Hospital as a concerned non-profit healthcare organisation. The Hospital maintains a team of doctors, well trained in multi-disciplinary areas of medicine and surgery. Together the men and women of the hospital strive to make each day more comfortable and promising for the patients.

The Lilavati Kirtilal Mehta Medical Trust is helping to spark a new confidence in India's ability to deliver a humane and affordable health care system. The Medical Centre will integrate outpatient and hospital care with research and education under a single roof, which will provide for greater efficiency in meeting the overall needs of a patient.

The Trust will continuously strive to improve the lives of the present and future generations through responsible application of medical technology in an environment of understanding and care.
