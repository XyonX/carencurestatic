Dr. Arvinder Singh Soin
Medanta Institute of Liver Transplantation and
Regenerative Medicine

Dr. Arvinder Singh Soin has performed nearly 1000 liver transplants in India, the maximum in the country. Liver transplantation team at Medanta currently performs 25 live donor liver transplants every month with 95% success results; at par with the world's best liver transplantaion centres. As a practice the team not only treats referrals from India; but also handles cases from the rest of South Asia, the Middle East and Africa
