Quick Enquiry

 

Privat Hospital is a 75 bed multi-specialty modern health care facility, which attempts to make the experience of a hospital stress free under the circumstances of illness or injuries. The hospital is on the M.G. Road just off the Delhi - Jaipur national Highway No.8 in Gurgaon. It is within a 15 min drive from the International Airport and about 35 min drive from the Diplomatic Enclave.

It was started by Dr. Kalyan S Sachdev in 1995 and has since grown in stature and reputation.

Hospital Infrastructure

Architectural design is unique with patient friendly plan and layout. It is surrounded by well maintained lawns and gardens providing an aesthetic and soothing touch of nature within its boundaries. A roof top lawn on the first floor has camouflaged portals for natural lighting of OPD and reception. Parking area within the boundary ensures an ease of parking for patients and other visitors.

The hospital operates under a high standard of Hygiene and Medical Care. It is clean, spacious and well lit. The corridors and patient rooms have also plenty of sunlight. The diagnosis and treatment of the patients is carried out by highly qualified Senior Physicians assisted by team of doctors and paramedical staff.
