Hospital Infrastructure

Quick Enquiry

Almost a quarter of a century old, Seven Hills Hospital is still amongst the top multi-specialty facilities in Vizag. This ISO certified 250-bed hospital provides healthcare covering a wide spectrum of medicine. Comprehensive cardiac care is provided here in terms of diagnostic, surgical and intensive care facilities. An exclusive cardiac care unit with state-of-the-art equipment is provided for patients undergoing procedures in the hospital.

The nephrology and urology departments treat diseases related to the kidney and the urinary system. Dialysis machines are available and even transplants are performed here. Gynecology and obstetrics, neurology, ophthalmology, ENT (ear, nose and throat), pediatrics, gastroenterology, plastic surgery, infertility treatment and trauma care are some of the other areas of treatment here. The hospital has a separate burn unit, neonatal intensive care unit, acute medical care unit, surgical intensive care unit etc. All these units have beds equipped with the latest monitoring equipment and ventilators. We were also told that the hospital's operation theatres boast the most modern fittings and support infrastructure.

Seven hills hospital healthcare services

Seven Hills offers comprehensive diagnostic services. Whether it is X-rays, CT scans, ultrasound tests or any other scan, they have hi-tech machines to accomplish these tasks. Their pathology lab too is geared to perform an array of tests. Their nursing staff comes from their own nursing school and they have a battery of consultants who are available both in the mornings and in the evenings. An in-house cafeteria meets the dietary requirements of patients. As far as patient accommodation is concerned, they can opt for beds in the general ward, non air conditioned or air conditioned single rooms, shared rooms and also luxury suites.

Like every big hospital today, Seven Hills too offers health packages. Their Comprehensive Health Check-Up costs Rs. 900 for men and Rs. 1,000 for women. It includes an examination by a physician, a battery of blood tests for hemoglobin, total cholesterol, total WBC (white blood corpuscles), glucose and many others. The women's check-up has all of the above plus a gynecological examination, ultrasound scan of the pelvis and a Pap smear. The Executive Health Check-Up costs Rs. 2,300 and is a more extensive package with lots more tests. The Child Check-Up program comes for Rs. 375, and includes a thorough examination by a pediatrician, urine analysis, chest X-ray and urine analysis among other tests.
