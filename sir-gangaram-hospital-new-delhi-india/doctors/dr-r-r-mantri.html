Dr. R.R. Mantri
MBBS, MD - General Medicine, DM - Cardiology,
DNB - Cardiology , Fellowship in
Electrophysiology and Pacemaker

Dr R.R Mantri is an esteemed Interventional Cardiologist, Diabetologist and Electrophysiologist, fully justifying is position of Vice-Chairman (Dept. of Cardiology) at Sir Ganga Ram Hospital, Delhi. He is an exceptionally qualified doctor with graduation and master's degrees from govt. recognized Jawaharlal Nehru Medical College, Ajmer. Not just that, besides DM and DNB in cardiology, he holds a Fellowship in Electrophysiology and Pacemaker from Mount Sinai School of Medicine, USA. His clinical interests include attending patients suffering from diabetes due to obesity, congenial heart diseases, cardiovascular diseases, coronary angioplasty, cardiac valve diseases, hypertension, heart failure, high cholesterol, radio frequency ablation and much, much more.

Dr R.R Mantri has dealt with the most complex of heart ailments in his 22 years of career. He dedicates his illustrious career to perseverance and medical training that he received in Japan, Brussels and Germany.

Right from commencing his education, Dr Mantri was an outstanding student. He carried this reputation and made his friends and family proud by winning Gold Medals in all subjects while pursuing MBBS and MD. He is very well acknowledged for his achievements in abroad as well, as he won The Best Challenging case award in USA and Paris. To his credit are more than 50 publications in widely read national and international journals.

Apart from foreseeing patients at Sir Ganga Ram Hospital, Delhi, Dr R.R Mantri is also available at his resident-cum-clinic in East Patel Nagar, Delhi.
