12 Best Cardiac Surgeons in Chennai, India 2025

The heart is a vital organ of the body and any problem related to the heart can be life threatening. Of course you may be aware of heart surgeries like heart transplant, Heart Value Repair and Replacement, Angioplasty, Mitral Value Repair and Replacement, Bypass Surgery etc. but there are many other surgeries that are conducted on this organ. Any kind of heart surgery requires a lot of skill and expertise heart surgeon and not to mention knowledge about the anatomy of the heart organ.Let us look at the different types of heart surgeries that are performed by skilled heart surgeons.

  • Aortic surgery which repairs or replaces the aortic dissections or aneurysms
  • Aortic valve surgery which replaces or repairs an aortic valve that is not functioning properly
  • Arrhythmia surgery which corrects irregular heart rhythms like atrial fibrillation or ventricular tachycardia
  • Congenital heart surgery which repairs the a genetic defect in the heart
  • Coronary artery bypass surgery which bypasses a block coronary artery with another piece of blood vessel
  • Left ventricular assist device which places a mechanical device in the heart to aid in the pumping of blood
  • Left ventricular remodeling – in a heart attack the shape of the heart gets disturbed and this surgery repairs the shape of the heart to allow it to pump blood more efficiently
  • Myectomy is performed to remove a portion of the enlarged wall of the heart
  • Valvular surgery is meant to repair or replace malfunctioning heart valves
  • Heart transplant is done in case of advanced heart failure in a situation where the heart cannot pump enough oxygenated blood to meet the needs of the body organs
