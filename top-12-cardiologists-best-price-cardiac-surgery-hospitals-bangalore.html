TOP 12 Cardiac Surgeon in Bangalore, India 2025

Cardiac surgery is a very complicated procedure and requires a lot of skill which the cardiac surgeon first qualifies for with education and then with experience. Usually surgeons who operate on the heart also perform surgeries on the other organs like lungs in the thoracic part of the body and hence they are known as cardio-thoracic surgeons. This is one of the most demanding and challenging areas of surgery. However, the work is rewarding in itself, because heart diseases are the major cause of death in the world and saving a human life by performing cardiac surgery provides immense satisfaction.

The main areas of cardiac surgery are – adult cardiac surgery, congenital cardiac surgery heart transplant surgery. Once the surgery is done it is very important to monitor patients in intensive care. Although nowadays cardiac surgery is safe, sometimes complications do occur. These include death, infection, stroke, heart rhythm problems, bleeding etc. the other surgeries performed by cardiac surgeon include coronary artery bypass surgery, heart valve surgery, and aortic surgery.
