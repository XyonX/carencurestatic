Dr. K.G Jayaprasanna
MS, Mch (CTVS - AIIMS)
Senior Consultant : Cardio Thoracic & Vascular Surgery

Dr. Jayaprasanna is one of the few to learn and practice Cardio - Thoracic and Vascular surgeries from renowned center within and outside India.

After obtaining training in Cardio-Thoracic & Vascular surgery from All India Institute of Medical Science New Delhi, he worked in the best centers like a Indraprastha Appolo Hospital Delhi, Royal Adelaide Hospital and The Prince Charles Hospital Brisbane, Australia. He has an exposure to more that 7500 Cardio-Thoracic and Vascular surgeries of which he has performed about 2500 surgeries independently. At Royal Adelaide Hospital he got expertise in complex aortic and minimally invasive Cardio-Thoracic surgeries.

EXPERTISE

  • Coronary Artery Bypass Surgery using multiple Arterial grafts.

  • Aortic Aneurysm Surgery

  • Congenital Cardiac Surgery

  • Pulmonary Resections

  • Video Assisted Thoracic Surgerie
